Our History

"For many years, Valery has been baking the most delicious products at her home, and sell organic food. In 2013, we opened our first store in Madrid, in a cosy little street near the Plaza Mayor. People from all over the world fell in love with our products... The store became a success in no time.

In 2015 no less than 1000 products were sold every day and we moved into a larger building.After receiving many requests, it became clear that our next step was making it possible to order from the comfort of your home and from anywhere in the world. You are now looking at the result of this: our very own webstore, making it possible to order whenever you feel like it!"
